Brizzle Bash

Darkness has crept into the corners of the world, leaving only faint glimmers of hope. At the center of this struggle is Oswald van Bristlewick, lovingly called Brizzly, the unshakeable defender of Moustacheshire. With relentless courage and a will of steel, Brizzly stands as the last line of defense against the looming threat of the Shadow Monsters.

Emerging from the depths of the Twilight Forest, the Shadow Monsters spread terror across the land. Townsfolk tremble at the thought of their ghostly forms and merciless attacks. But Brizzly, with unmatched agility, combat expertise, and an unwavering sense of justice, refuses to let darkness claim the world. He is Moustacheshire’s final hope to restore peace.

All Characters

Movement

A - Hold the A key to move to the left.
D - Hold the D key to move to the right.
S - Hold the S key to duck or crouch.
W - Press the W key to make the character jump.
␣ - Press the Spacebar to make the character jump.
⇧ - Hold Shift to run faster during gameplay.

Attacks

J - Press the J key to unleash a quick, close-range power punch.
K - Press the K key to launch a long-range attack with a spiky iron ball.

Collectibles

- Pick up hearts to heal your character and restore health.
- Pick up bombs to unleash special attacks with devastating power.

Utility Keys

P - Press P to break or return to the game.
